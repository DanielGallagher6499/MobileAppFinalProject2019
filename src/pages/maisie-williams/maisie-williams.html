Maisie Williams

Maisie Williams is an English actress. In 2011, she made her professional acting debut as Arya Stark in the HBO fantasy television series Game of Thrones (2011). Williams has also had a recurring role in Doctor Who (2005) as Ashildr in 2015. In addition to television, she made her feature film debut in the mystery The Falling (2014), for which she won the London Film Critics' Circle Award for Young Performer of the Year. In 2012, Williams played Loren Caleigh in the BBC series The Secret of Crickley Hall (2012). She also appeared in the independent films Heatstroke (2013) and Gold (2014), and the short films Corvidae (2018) and Up on the Roof (2013).
